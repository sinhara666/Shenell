📌 Lesson 1: What is AI? ▼

AI stands for Artificial Intelligence. It's software that can understand human language and respond intelligently. Think of it like texting a really smart assistant who never sleeps and is always free. Tools like Groq let you use AI without paying anything.

📌 Lesson 2: What is Groq? ▼

Groq is a free AI platform. You get an API key (like a password) that lets you connect to their AI from any tool or app. It's faster than ChatGPT and completely free. Go to console.groq.com to get yours.

📌 Lesson 3: What can I do with AI? ▼

Write emails, answer questions, summarize documents, create social media posts, build chatbots, automate tasks, generate business ideas, learn new topics fast, and much more — all for free.

📌 Lesson 4: How do I use this dashboard? ▼

1. Get your free Groq key at console.groq.com. 2. Paste it in the key box at the top. 3. Go to "Ask AI" and start chatting. 4. Use "Practice" to try real AI tasks. That's it — you're an AI user now!
